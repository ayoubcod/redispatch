Research & Inquiries

REdispatch is a technical project focused on developing deterministic engineering specifications for the energy transition.

Educational Focus

Engineering models should be auditable. The goal is clear, rule-based methodologies that connect energy physics, techno-economics, and carbon accounting without black boxes.

Future Development

The REdispatch suite is in a specification-first development phase. Priority is technical correctness, explicit assumptions, and reproducible methodology before any public distribution.
